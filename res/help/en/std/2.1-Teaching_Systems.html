Teaching Systems

Teaching Systems are a mobile learning environment, which allows a teacher to remotely access a students monitor without a IT experts support.

You can also safely make exams, homeworks and tests in the exam environment.

Tipp: The Teaching System is not equally to the exam environment.

The Teaching System is an add-in to the normal Lernstick, the exam environment is a reduced version of the Lernstick, which is not used during class.
